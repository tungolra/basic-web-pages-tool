This particular page is about links! There are three kinds of links:

  • Absolute links, like to Mozilla Developer Network, which is a very good resource for web developers.
  • Relative links link to our extras page.
    • Relative links are nice because they let you move around entire folders without having to update all the href’s on your elements, but they can get a little confusing when all your links start with a bunch of dots. They’re best for referring to resources in the same folder or in a standalone section of your website.
